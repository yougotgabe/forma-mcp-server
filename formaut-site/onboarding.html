Your site needs a home.

Three free accounts — GitHub, Cloudflare, and Supabase. Each one takes about two minutes. Your site runs on these accounts permanently, even if you stop using Formaut. We're setting up your infrastructure, not renting you ours.

🔒 Credentials are encrypted before storage. API tokens you enter here are encrypted the moment they're received — the raw value is never stored, never logged, and never visible to anyone, including us. Only the encrypted form is saved. Tokens are used only to build and deploy your site.
