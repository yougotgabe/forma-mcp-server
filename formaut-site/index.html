Why this exists

Built by someone who
needed it and couldn't

I built Formaut because I was building a website and the whole time I kept thinking: there has to be a better way. Developers are expensive and slow. Platforms lock you in. Templates look like templates. And none of them give you anything you actually own when you're done paying.

But the deeper frustration was different. I had AI. I could talk through ideas, get code, get copy, get strategy. What I couldn't get was an agent that stayed in the room until the thing was actually done. One that was connected to my real tools — my hosting, my database, my domain — and could do the wiring, not just hand me instructions and wish me luck.

I didn't just want help thinking. I wanted help doing.

And underneath all of it was something that bothered me more the longer I thought about it: every subscription I was paying for — the website platform, the booking tool, the payment processor — was another landlord. Another company that owned a piece of my online presence and could change the terms, raise the price, or lock me out whenever they felt like it.

Formaut is the answer to all three of those frustrations. An agent connected to infrastructure you own. One that finishes the job instead of handing you instructions. One that costs you $50 to build something worth hundreds — and then gets out of the way unless you need it.

I built this because it's a tool I wished existed. Every design decision — the accounts in your name, the site that keeps running when you cancel, the admin panel that's yours forever — comes from that same place. I wanted independence from platforms that profit from your dependency. Formaut is what that looks like.

Honest answers

The questions
worth asking.

What happens if I cancel?
Your site keeps running. Forever. It's on your Cloudflare account, your GitHub repo, your Supabase database. We're not in the loop. You just stop paying us and everything continues exactly as it was.
Do I need to know anything about websites?
Nothing. If you can describe your business in plain English, Formaut can build the site. The technical setup takes about ten minutes and we walk you through every step with screenshots.
Why do I need to buy a domain separately?
Because it should belong to you, not us. About $10 a year through Cloudflare Registrar gets you an address on the internet that's registered in your name. We help you get it and connect it in minutes. That $10 is one of the best business investments you'll make.
What if I come back after canceling?
Reconnect your accounts and Formaut picks up exactly where you left off. Your conversation history, site preferences, and business context are stored in your own Supabase — they never leave.
What is Claude and why does it power Formaut?
Claude is an AI built by Anthropic — the same company behind some of the most capable AI systems in the world. Formaut uses Claude because it's exceptional at understanding plain language requests and translating them into working, deployed websites. We're transparent about this because we think you should know what's powering your tools.
Can Formaut build anything?
Most things a small business needs — yes. There are some complex custom features that go beyond what Formaut handles automatically. When that happens, we tell you clearly and log it as a feature request. We never pretend we can do something we can't.
Is my data safe?
Your data lives in your own Supabase account — not ours. We access it to help build and maintain your site, but we don't own it, can't sell it, and it doesn't disappear if something happens to us.
I already pay for Claude or ChatGPT. Is Formaut redundant?
Not at all — and Formaut is complete on its own without them. Every plan gives you everything needed for a fully operating website. If you already use other AI tools, you can use them to workshop ideas before bringing them to Formaut — that keeps your AI focused on building and can yield richer results. But Formaut alone is never lacking.
Can I export my data to use with another AI tool?
Yes. You can download your Formaut context — business details, site decisions, preferences, conversation history — as a formatted file you can paste into Claude, ChatGPT, or any other AI. Your knowledge is portable. We never lock it in.
Will I be able to use my Claude subscription with Formaut?
That's exactly where we're headed. Anthropic is building toward letting Claude.ai connect directly to external tools and services. When that opens up, you'll be able to use the Claude app on your phone or desktop to build and maintain your site through Formaut — your existing subscription as the interface, Formaut's infrastructure doing the building. We'll ship it the moment it's available.
What's the intake form the agent offers?
Early in your first conversation, Formaut will offer to send you a short form — business name, location, phone, pages you need. It's the information that doesn't really need a conversation to figure out. Fill it in and Formaut goes straight to building. Ignore it and just keep talking — either way your site gets built.
How long does it take to get my site live?
Most sites go live in a single conversation — usually under an hour. The account setup takes about ten minutes the first time. After that, one conversation with Formaut and your site is deployed to your domain. Faster than any alternative, and when it's done, you own it outright.
